Careers in Computer Science

Someone who graduates with a degree in the field of computer science can have many different job titles in the industry. Building software, managing online databases, protecting your clients from a cyber attack, and maximizing computer effecieny are just a few examples of where this degree could take you. Click on one of the job names listed below to learn more about each job title in greater detail!

    Computer scientists with a focus on designing and analyzing the overall structure and functionality of computer systems. Click here to learn more about computer architects!

    A database administrator is a computer scientist that has a specialization in working with databases. They will maintain, install, and handle any other problems a database might have. Click here to learn more about database administrators!

    Cybersecurity professionals are computer scientists who specialize in the protection of their clients technology and data. Click here to learn more about cybersecurity professionals!

    Software engineers are computer scientists who specialize in writing computer programs. Click here to learn more about software engineers!
