Cyber Security


Computer scientists that are professionals in cybersecurity specialize in protecting machins, databases, and much more. Cyber professionals take the responibility of detecting threats, designing a security infrastructure, and responding to incidents and breaches with haste

Threat Detection/Prevention

Network monitoring, analyzing logs, and using other threat detection tools are just a little bit of what goes into threat detection for a cyber specialist. They do everything that they can to prevent a breach from happening.

Security Infrastructure

Designing. implementing, and maintaining a strong secruity infrastructure is a big part of a cyber specialist's job. They configure firewalls, creat encryptions, and create much more to defend the systems they are protecting.

Incident Response and Recovery

Cyber specialists try their absolute best to prevent breaches but no one is perfect. Sooner or later a breach will occur and these specialists have to do the best damage control possible while also trying to regain control of their system.
